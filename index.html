A note from us. Know Your Area is an independent civic-tech initiative built to help citizens understand their neighbourhood — public infrastructure, elected representatives and government schemes — using freely available open data. Information shown here is aggregated from third-party sources (OpenStreetMap, Nominatim, RTI Wiki, sansad.in, etc.) and may be incomplete, out of date, or incorrect. It is intended for general awareness only and must not be relied upon as legal proof, official record, or for any official, financial or legal proceeding. Always verify with the relevant government authority before acting on anything you see here.
